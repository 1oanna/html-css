by ColorFull

Ever since I remember my self, I've been playing with a camera. Beginning with photo films developed by hand in my dark room and then transitioning to digital photography. I like taking photos that capture details, that human eyes cannot see. In both, macro-photography and telephotography, I meet my need of searching beyond the obvious. Hand held captions, taking me to the moon and the sun, the magnificent colors of sunset and sunrise on the one hand and on the other hidden in the macro-world of bugs, insects, butterflies and flowers and the beauty of tiny creatures that live among us unnoticed. Outdoor shooting, when natural light changes and a slight breeze can change the whole scenery, when all subjects are in constant move. After 200 shots, there maybe that one, worth waiting for. Unprocessed, without filters, photo editing and manipulation programs, only capturing that little moment that will be forever gone. A never ending game, which I adore.
