in this section you will find some information about me

Creative and vigilant individuals need a watchful lawyer who understands their passions, who feels their needs and can give them solutions and new perspectives through the complicated maze of their legal worries. There is where I come in to help you built your dreams.
My name is Mary Talasli, I am an Attorney of law living in Syros, a greek island of the Aegean sea.
Focusing on legal needs of land investors in Greece, as land is one of the best investments somebody can make, especially in a place where sea and sun dominate.
After graduating with honors from Syros’ First General Senior High School in 1993, I earned a legal degree at the School of Law Economics and Political Sciences of the University of Athens in 1999. As I wanted to look into the law science, I pursued a Master’s Program of Legal Sociology in 2001.
From June 2001 till today I am a member of Syros Bar Association, practicing Private Law (Civil Law, Contract Law, Family Law), Public Law (Criminal Law, Administrative Law, Immigration Law, Construction Law).
My clients include foreigners and greek land investors both individuals and companies, as I speak fluently English and French.
After practicing private law for years I decided on 2013 to do a Training in Mediation and Alternative Dispute Resolution, becoming an Accredited Mediator from the Greek Ministry of Justice. Public Administration is always an interesting field to deal with so on 2016 I got a Master’s Degree at Panteion University of Social and Political Sciences in Athens.
